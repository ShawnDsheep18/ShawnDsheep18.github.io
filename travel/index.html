Tilicho Lake, Nepal

Tilicho Lake, located in the Annapurna range of the Himalayas in Nepal, is one of the highest lakes in the world, situated at an altitude of 4,919 meters (16,138 feet). Its stunning blue waters surrounded by snow-capped peaks make it a popular destination for trekkers and adventure enthusiasts.
